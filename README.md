# PolicyKit-Quote
A fast, mobile-first motor insurance quote form — 3 steps, smart NCB derivation, and invisible fraud signals. Built as a product assessment.

# Steps to Access the Form

1. Open your browser and go to https://hkgautam.github.io/PolicyKit-Quote/
2. You'll land directly on the quote form — no login or setup needed.
3. On the first screen, enter a vehicle registration number in the field and click Look up.

  The following registration numbers have vehicle data built in:
  
  DL09CA1234 — Maruti Suzuki Swift VXi, 2021, Petrol, Delhi
  MH01AB5678 — Hyundai Creta SX(O), 2022, Diesel, Mumbai
  KA03XY9012 — Tata Nexon XZ+, 2023, Electric (EV), Bengaluru


4. After a successful lookup, the vehicle details appear automatically. Click Continue to Coverage.
5. On Step 2, answer the two NCB questions and set your IDV preference, then click See Quote Range.
6. On Step 3, enter your name, mobile number, email, and date of birth to see the final quote. Click View My Quotes.

Note: Any other registration number will trigger the manual entry flow where you fill in make, model, variant, year, and fuel type yourself.
