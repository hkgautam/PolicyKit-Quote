# PolicyKit-Quote
A fast, mobile-first motor insurance quote form — 3 steps, smart NCB derivation, and invisible fraud signals. Built as a product assessment.

Steps to Access the Form

Open your browser and go to https://<your-github-username>.github.io/policykit-quote
You'll land directly on the quote form — no login or setup needed.
On the first screen, enter a vehicle registration number in the field and click Look up.

The following registration numbers have vehicle data built in:

DL09CA1234 — Maruti Suzuki Swift VXi, 2021, Petrol, Delhi
MH01AB5678 — Hyundai Creta SX(O), 2022, Diesel, Mumbai
KA03XY9012 — Tata Nexon XZ+, 2023, Electric (EV), Bengaluru


After a successful lookup, the vehicle details appear automatically. Click Continue to Coverage.
On Step 2, answer the two NCB questions and set your IDV preference, then click See Quote Range.
On Step 3, enter your name, mobile number, email, and date of birth to see the final quote. Click View My Quotes.

Any other registration number will trigger the manual entry flow where you fill in make, model, variant, year, and fuel type yourself.
